Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Alfred processes everything on your device. No audio, transcripts, or personal data is ever sent to external servers. We don't use analytics or tracking of any kind.

Which devices are supported?

Alfred requires iOS 26 or later and an Apple Intelligence-capable device (iPhone 15 Pro or later). AI-powered features including action extraction, daily summaries, and the chatbot require Apple Intelligence.

How much does Alfred cost?

Alfred is a one-time purchase for $1.99. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no hidden fees. You pay once and get full access to all features, including future updates.

How is Alfred different from Otter.ai or other transcription apps?

Unlike cloud-based alternatives, Alfred processes everything on your device — your conversations never leave your phone. Plus, Alfred automatically extracts action items, decisions, and reminders from your conversations, a feature no competitor offers. And at $1.99 one-time vs $16.99/month for Otter, Alfred is significantly more affordable.

Why does Alfred need microphone access?

The microphone is used to record conversations for transcription. Audio is processed on-device and stored locally. You can delete recordings at any time.

Can I use Alfred in the background?

Yes. Alfred supports background audio recording, so you can continue recording while using other apps.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual sessions from the recordings list, or delete all data from Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Is the transcription accurate?

Transcription accuracy depends on audio quality, background noise, and language. For best results, keep the device within 3-4 feet of speakers in a reasonably quiet environment. AI-generated summaries and actions should always be reviewed for accuracy.

Does Alfred support Family Sharing?

Yes. Alfred supports Apple Family Sharing, allowing up to 6 family members to use the app from a single $1.99 purchase.

Can I request a refund?

All purchases are handled by Apple. If you'd like a refund, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com within 14 days of purchase.
