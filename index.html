Alfred On-Device AI Assistant

Your private, on-device AI meeting assistant for iOS.

What is Alfred?

Alfred listens to your conversations, transcribes them in real-time, and intelligently extracts action items, decisions, and summaries — all processed entirely on your device. Your data never leaves your phone.

Key Features

  • On-device transcription — Real-time speech-to-text using Apple's Speech framework
  • AI-powered analysis — Extract actions, decisions, and summaries using Apple Intelligence
  • Speaker identification — Distinguish between different speakers in conversations
  • Reminders & Calendar sync — Send extracted items to iOS Reminders and Calendar
  • Complete privacy — All processing happens on your device, no data sent to servers
  • 10 languages supported — Turkish, English, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Italian

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Alfred processes everything on your device. No audio, transcripts, or personal data is ever sent to external servers. We don't use analytics or tracking of any kind.

Which devices are supported?

Alfred requires iOS 18 or later. AI features powered by Apple Intelligence require iPhone 15 Pro or later. On older devices, the app falls back to rule-based analysis.

Why does Alfred need microphone access?

The microphone is used to record conversations for transcription. Audio is processed on-device and stored locally. You can delete recordings at any time.

Can I use Alfred in the background?

Yes. Alfred supports background audio recording, so you can continue recording while using other apps.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual sessions from the recordings list, or delete all data from Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Is the transcription accurate?

Transcription accuracy depends on audio quality, background noise, and language. For best results, keep the device close to speakers in a quiet environment. AI-generated summaries and actions should always be reviewed for accuracy.
