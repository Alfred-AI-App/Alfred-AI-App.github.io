Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Alfred is built with privacy at its core. Alfred uses only Apple's own frameworks for AI processing — Apple Intelligence and Apple's Speech framework. No data is sent to third-party AI services such as OpenAI or Google. When AI features are enabled, your data may be processed by Apple via on-device processing or Apple's Private Cloud Compute with end-to-end encryption.

1. Overview

Alfred ("the App") is an AI-powered personal assistant for iOS that records conversations, transcribes speech, and extracts actionable insights. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

The App is developed and maintained by an independent developer ("we", "us", "our"). By using Alfred, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect or transmit any personal data to our own servers
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry services
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties
  • We do not use third-party AI services (no OpenAI, no Google, no external AI APIs)
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage statistics

3. AI Data Processing

Alfred uses Apple's frameworks for AI features. Before any AI processing occurs, the App requests your explicit consent through a dedicated AI Data Processing consent screen. You can enable or disable AI data processing at any time from Settings.

Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models)

When AI features are enabled, your transcribed text and chat messages are processed by Apple Intelligence to generate summaries, extract action items, categorize sessions, and provide chat responses. Apple Intelligence may process data in two ways:

  • On-device processing: Data is processed locally on your device using Apple's on-device language models.
  • Private Cloud Compute: For more complex requests, Apple Intelligence may use Apple's Private Cloud Compute servers. This processing uses end-to-end encryption — Apple cannot access your data, data is not stored after processing, and data is never used to train AI models.

Apple Speech Recognition

Audio is transcribed to text using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. When on-device speech recognition is available, transcription happens locally. When on-device recognition is not available for the selected language, audio may be sent to Apple's speech recognition servers for processing.

Speaker Identification (FluidAudio)

The App uses a bundled machine learning model (FluidAudio, based on pyannote) to distinguish between different speakers in a conversation. This model runs entirely on your device with no network communication.

What Data Is Sent Where

  • To Apple (via Apple Intelligence): Transcribed text, chat messages — only when AI features are enabled and you have given consent
  • To Apple (via Speech Recognition): Audio data — only when on-device recognition is unavailable
  • To third parties: Nothing. No data is ever sent to any third party.

4. Data Processed On Your Device

The following data is always processed and stored locally on your device:

Audio Recordings

When you start a listening session, the App records audio through your device's microphone. Audio files are stored locally in the App's sandboxed storage.

Transcripts, Actions, and Summaries

All transcripts, extracted action items, decisions, and summaries are stored locally in an encrypted database on your device.

Chat Conversations

Conversations with the in-app AI assistant are stored locally on your device.

5. User Consent for AI Features

Alfred implements an explicit consent flow for AI data processing:

  • During onboarding, users are informed about AI data processing and asked for consent
  • Before any AI feature is used, consent must be granted
  • The consent clearly discloses: what data is processed, who processes it (Apple), and how it is protected
  • Users can revoke consent at any time from Settings > Apple Intelligence > AI Data Processing
  • Without consent, AI features (summaries, action extraction, AI chat) are disabled, but recording and transcription continue to work

6. Data Storage and Security

All locally stored data is protected with the following security measures:

  • Encrypted database: Your transcripts, actions, and summaries are stored in a database protected by iOS file protection (encrypted at rest)
  • Keychain protection: Encryption keys are stored in the iOS Keychain with device-only access
  • App sandboxing: All files are stored within the App's iOS sandbox, inaccessible to other apps
  • Optional biometric lock: You can enable Face ID or Touch ID to protect access to the App

7. Device Permissions

Alfred requests the following device permissions, each requiring your explicit consent:

  • Microphone: Required to record conversations for transcription
  • Speech Recognition: Required for speech-to-text conversion
  • Reminders: Optional — allows you to sync extracted action items to the iOS Reminders app
  • Calendar: Optional — allows you to sync extracted events to the iOS Calendar app
  • Notifications: Optional — for daily summary and morning briefing alerts
  • Siri: Optional — enables voice shortcuts to control the App hands-free

8. Integration with iOS Apps

When you choose to sync an action item to iOS Reminders or Calendar, the relevant data (title, description, date) is written to those apps via Apple's EventKit framework. This data then follows the privacy settings of your Reminders and Calendar apps, including any iCloud sync you may have enabled at the system level. Alfred itself does not initiate any cloud synchronization.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual listening sessions, transcripts, or action items at any time
  • Delete all data from Settings
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data

10. Purchase and Payment Data

Alfred is available as a one-time purchase ($1.99) through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID data. For information about how Apple handles your purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

11. Children's Privacy

Alfred is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

12. Third-Party Services

Alfred does not integrate with any third-party AI services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The only frameworks used are:

  • Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models): For text analysis — provided by Apple Inc.
  • Apple Speech Recognition: For speech-to-text — provided by Apple Inc.
  • FluidAudio (pyannote): For speaker identification — bundled on-device, open-source, no network communication
