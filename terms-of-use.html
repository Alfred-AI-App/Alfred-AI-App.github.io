2. Description of the App

Alfred is an AI-powered personal assistant for iOS that provides the following features:

  • Recording and transcribing conversations using on-device speech recognition
  • Analyzing transcripts to extract action items, decisions, and summaries using on-device AI
  • Identifying different speakers in conversations
  • Optionally syncing extracted items to iOS Reminders and Calendar
  • Providing an AI chat assistant for questions about your recorded sessions

All processing occurs entirely on your device. The App does not transmit your data to external servers.
